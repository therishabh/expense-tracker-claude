Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2026

Spendly ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights over it.

1. Information We Collect

When you create an account or use Spendly, we may collect:

  • Account information — your name, email address, and password (stored as a secure hash).
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter while tracking expenses.
  • Usage data — pages visited, features used, and timestamps, to help us improve the product.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain the Spendly service.
  • Authenticate your identity and keep your account secure.
  • Send transactional emails (e.g., password resets) — no marketing without consent.
  • Analyse aggregated, anonymised usage patterns to improve the app.

3. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored on servers located in India. We apply industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS), hashed passwords, and access controls. No method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties except:

  • When required by law or a valid legal process.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of Spendly, our users, or the public.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at the address below. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Cookies

Spendly uses a single session cookie to keep you logged in. It is strictly necessary for the service to function and is removed when you log out or close your browser.

7. Children's Privacy

Spendly is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at privacy@spendly.app.
